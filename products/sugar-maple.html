Sugar Maple

Sugar Maple (Acer saccharin) also known as Rock Maple or Hard Maple, ranges in color from a cream white to gold. It is sourced primarily from the Northeastern United States and Canada. This unique wood contributes subtle notes of maple syrup, with a soft sweetness. Additional hints of vanilla, caramel and Bourbon pecan can also be detected. This wood is incomparable to most because it provides balance in mouthfeel, durability and creaminess.
