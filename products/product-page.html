American Oak

American Oak

American Oak (Quercus Alba / White Oak) is selected from Northern America Forrests. Our American Oak provides notes, depending on toast level, of sweetness, vanilla, coffee, hazelnut, caramel, fresh baked breads and provides an elegant tannin structure.

Barrel Packs Infusion Spirals

(9” x 1.5”) 1 set Includes 6 Spirals linked together by a food grade nylon mesh. The mesh not only permits liquids to freely contact all surfaces of each spiral, it also creates an ergonometric value- the spirals are easily retrievable if fastened to the bung ( or other alternative ) at the end of Barrel.

Tank Pack Infusion Spirals

(48” x 1.5”) 1 set Includes 5 Spirals linked together by a food grade nylon mesh. The mesh not only permits liquids to freely contact all surfaces of each spiral, it also creates an ergonometric value- the spirals are easily retrievable if fastened to the bung ( or other alternative ) at the end of Tank.

Carboy Infusion Spirals

(8” x 1”) 1 set Includes 2 Spirals. The carboy or “demijohn” spirals are convenient for smaller productions and minimal diameter insertion space.

Bottle Spirals

(2.5” x 5/8”) Includes 1 Bottle Spiral. These spirals will fit into any 750ml bottle neck. They can be used in a choice of beer, bourbon, whiskey, rum, tequila, wines, etc., to increase the oak and smoky flavors that the American Oak Charred #3 toast provides.
